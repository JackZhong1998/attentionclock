**Часы Фокуса** is a free, open-source Mac focus timer. Set a session (default 25 min), work without distractions, and track your daily progress.

No account or internet required. All data stays on your Mac.

## Why you'll like it

- **One tap to start**
- **Clean, calm design**
- **Flexible timing** (default 25 min)
- **Pause, resume, or end anytime**
- **Track progress** with trees 🌳 and a heatmap
- **Desktop companions (optional)** — 3000+ downloadable pixel characters with floating window
- **Completely free** and open source

## Desktop companions (optional)

Focus doesn't have to feel lonely. With desktop companions you can:

- **Browse 3000+ characters** — search the catalog and download favorites
- **Get reactions while focusing** — your companion stays beside the timer
- **Float on your desktop** — keep your companion visible across apps
- **Turn off anytime** — the timer works perfectly on its own

## How to use

1. Download and install
2. Open **Часы Фокуса** from Applications
3. Tap **Start Focus**
4. Get a notification when done

## Install

1. Open the `.dmg` file
2. Double-click **Install and Open**
3. If blocked, see **First Launch Guide.txt** in the DMG

### Cannot verify Install and Open?

1. Click **Done** if blocked
2. System Settings → **Open Anyway** for **Install and Open**
3. Double-click **Install and Open** again

### Attention Clock “damaged”?

1. Click **Cancel**
2. System Settings → **Open Anyway** for Attention Clock
3. Open the app again

## FAQ

**Blocked?**  
Allow Install and Open, then Attention Clock.

**Internet required?**  
No, runs entirely on your Mac.

**Where is data stored?**  
Locally on your Mac only.

**Windows or iPhone?**  
Mac only, macOS 14.0+.
